# Privacy Policy for Scoreboard App

## Overview
**Scoreboard App** is committed to "High Integrity Privacy." We respect your privacy and are dedicated to protecting any personal information that may be involved during your use of the app. This policy explains our approach to data collection, usage, and local storage.

By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

## Information Collection and Local Storage
**All match data, team names, score history, and game settings are stored strictly locally on your device.** 
*   We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your scoring or team data on external servers.
*   The data is stored using local device storage (`shared_preferences` and local database) and remains under your control.

## Third-Party Store Information
We do receive certain non-personal information from the Google Play Store and Apple App Store, including:
*   Download metrics and installation statistics.
*   Ratings and written reviews.
*   Anonymized crash reports (to help us identify and fix technical issues).

We use this information solely to improve the app's performance and respond to user feedback.

## Analytics and Tracking
To understand how our website is used and to improve the user experience, we utilize Google Tag Manager (GTM). GTM allows us to deploy and manage various tracking tags, primarily for anonymous, aggregated analytics.

*   **What we collect:** GTM is configured to collect non-personal, anonymized data related to website usage, such as page views, session duration, and general interaction patterns on [scoreboard.shahadat.us](https://scoreboard.shahadat.us/).
*   **What we DO NOT collect:** We explicitly do not use GTM to collect any personal identifying information, team names, score records, or any other sensitive user-generated content. All such data remains strictly local to your device as described in the "Information Collection and Local Storage" section.
*   **Purpose:** The data collected via GTM is used solely for internal analysis to enhance the website's performance, identify areas for improvement, and optimize content.

This usage of GTM is in line with our commitment to "High Integrity Privacy" and ensures that your personal data remains private and secure.

## Use of Information
The local data on your device is used strictly for the app's functionality (keeping score, tracking match timers, and displaying session history). Information received from app stores and anonymous analytics data collected via Google Tag Manager are used to improve the overall user experience, ensure platform stability, and understand general usage patterns.

## Disclosure of Information
We do not disclose your information to third parties. We do not sell or trade any data. Information is only disclosed if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

## Data Retention and Deletion
*   **User Data:** Since all data is stored locally, deleting the Scoreboard App from your device (or clearing your browser cache for the web version) will permanently remove all score history and settings.
*   **Store Data:** We do not retain personal identifiers from store-provided metrics.

## Permissions
*   **Internet Access:** Required only for the web version to load assets and to provide access to our official GitHub support and documentation pages.
*   **Storage:** Required to persist your team colors, names, and match history across app sessions.
*   **Haptics/Audio:** Used to provide tactile and audible feedback (whistle) during match updates.

## Security
We employ modern development practices to ensure your local data is handled securely. However, please remember that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we recommend using device-level security (passcodes/biometrics) to protect your hardware.

## Children's Privacy
Scoreboard App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children. If you are under 13, please do not use the app.

## Changes to This Policy
We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Updates will be indicated by a new version number or date at the top of the policy.

## Contact Us
If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or our high-integrity data practices, please get in touch with us via the **Issues** section of our GitHub repository.
