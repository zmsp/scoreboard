## Why use Scoreboard?
We traded complex menus for a "heads-up" design that lets you focus on the court, not your screen.

* **Feel the Game**: Instant updates with haptic feedback and referee sounds—you'll know the point registered without looking down.
* **The Big Picture**: A persistent timeline tracks every point, so you can settle the "what was the score two minutes ago?" debate instantly.
* **Modern Aesthetics**: A sleek dark mode using glassmorphism and color palettes inspired by international team colors.
* **Wrist Control**: Real-time syncing with Apple Watch for score and timer management on the fly.
* **Total Privacy**: No accounts, no cloud, no tracking. Your match data stays on your device and nowhere else.
* **Court-Ready**: Optimized to look stunning in fullscreen on tablets or phones at the sidelines.
